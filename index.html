Install Addons

  • Open website Download CurserForge App in browser.
  • Download Windows version of CurseForge standalone (not CurseForge on Overwolf).
  • Run the downloaded CurseForge Windows - Installer.exe.
  • Check I accept the Terms of use and Privacy Policy and I confirm I'm at least 18 years old when asked to do so.
  • On the screen We value your privacy click the Manage button.
  • Click Reject All Purposes button then uncheck all Legitimate interest purposes.
  • Click Save and Exit button.
  • Click Skip intro button.
  • Click Continue as guest.
  • If you get A new Update is ready click Update button.
  • Open Settings page of CurseForge app.
  • In the General > CurseForge > When I close CurseForge section choose Exit CurseForge.
  • In the General > Privacy > Discord Rich Presence uncheck Show your activity status on Discord.
  • In the General > Privacy > How we use your data uncheck Optimize performance and stability.
  • In the General > Privacy > How we use your data uncheck Customize your experience.
  • Close Settings page of CurseForge app.
  • In Home > Choose a Game page of CurseForge app click World of Warcraft.
  • Click Browse for Addons button.
